After completing my bachelor's degree in social sciences at Vrije Universiteit Brussel with a specialization in communication sciences, I developed a strong passion for digital media. To further pursue this interest, I earned two master's degrees: one in Digital Media and Society at KU Leuven University and another in Digital Media in Europe at Vrije Universiteit Brussel. I'm committed to staying up-to-date with the latest trends and developments in digital media and online environments. Recently, I gained valuable experience working as a Social Media Officer on the IFRC's Programmatic Partnership with the EU.
